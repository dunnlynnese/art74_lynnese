Infinity Song

Summary

Infinity Song is a soft rock band consisting of four members. The members are siblings signed under roc nation. They began performing in 2014 as a three sibling band consisting of Victory, Angel and Abraham Boyd. Later introducing the siblings Israel and Momo Boyd. Victory has since left the group leaving the four siblings Abraham, Angel, Israel and Momo. They have several albums but the songs that have captivated me the most comes from their newest album Metamorphosis. The five songs

1. Pink Sky
2. Lotus
3. Sinking Boat
4. Comedy
5. Metamorphosis
